ABOUT MADINATY AL-ALAMIA

MADINATY AL-ALAMIA is a well-balanced electromechanical and engineering-based solutions provider. Our strengths are derived from our proven ability to leverage our most valuable assets — our people, suppliers, technology, capabilities, and our unrelenting focus on creating value for our customers.

Throughout our history, we have been a wellspring of innovation, creativity, and technological advances. We are proud to say that Madinaty AL-Alamia plans to continue its heritage of leadership in the markets we serve and in the communities where we live and work.

Madinaty AL-Alamia has successfully transitioned to become a trusted electromechanical solution provider in Egypt and KSA.

Many projects have trusted Madinaty AL-Alamia to deliver sustainable and turnkey solutions. From commercial facilities to residential projects, malls to administrative buildings and hospitals, we give full attention to every detail in all our projects.

It is satisfying to know that we are able to help our clients with strategic solutions for their requirements. Customer satisfaction is the hallmark by which we measure our performance, and we hold ourselves — as do our clients — to the highest standards of quality.

In this continually evolving marketplace, our clients are more informed than ever about their solution provider options. Even so, they continue to select Madinaty AL-Alamia as their partner of choice because of our experience, commitment to quality, community, and integrity.

I hope this provides you with a few reasons to get to know Madinaty AL-Alamia better.
